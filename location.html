Sights &places you should see

Why Bhubaneswar?

The first edition of this hackathon was organised in Bhubaneswar as HackDAV and it has been organised in the city annually ever since. This is the first time we are accepting international applications and wanted everyone to get a glimpse of the rich culture of the city before we start changing the venue every year.

Travel Information

By Plane

If you are flying to StellarHacks, we recommend arriving at Biju Patnaik International Airport (BBI) a day earlier. The venue and your place of stay will be just a cab-ride from the airport. Get in touch with us in advance and we will facilitate your stay.
Biju Patnaik International Airport - BBI

By Train

If you are arriving by train, book your train till the Bhubaneswar Railway Station (BBS). Trains are subject to delays and we would suggest choosing an arrival time well before the commencement of the hackathon.
Bhubaneswar Railway Station - BBS
